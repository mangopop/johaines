About

/bɪˈspəʊk/

bSpK jewellery design was created as a showcase for the work of goldsmith and Jewellery designer Jo Moorshead. Jo studied art and design at Wiltshire college during which time she became interested in jewellery design. This led to a bachelor of arts degree in jewellery at Middlesex university in London. During her university career Jo worked with modern materials such as acrylic and resin, and displayed work at various shows including ‘new designers’ in Islington. After graduating with honours Jo worked in jewellery workshops in Bath and Bradford on Avon. Jo now works from a private workshop creating handmade bSpK jewellery from engagement and wedding rings to dress and formalwear.
